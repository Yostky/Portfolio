ABOUT ME

Hello there!

My name is Kyle Yost, and I am self taught in computer programming.

For most of my life, I wanted to be a Physical Therapist because of my fascination with the structure and capabilities of the human body. Once I graduated college with Bachelors in Kinesiology, I was realised that my passion for the human body did not outweight the decades of crippling debt that gradute school would force upon me. So, I was faced with a life changing decision - what do I do now?

For the past year of my life, I have dedicated myself to learn the structure and capabilties of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I chose these languages because I thought they would be easy to start with and because front-end engineering was very interesting to me. Now that I am a year into this, I feel like I have the necessary skills and understanding to become a software engineer.

Weather App

This weather app is built with all free resourses, and is coded with HTML, CSS, and vanilla Javascript. This project taught me about API calls, event listeners, regular expressions, and how Javascript is able to manipulate HTML and CSS. Working on this app also taught me the importance of clean code, as I found it very difficult to navigate lines of code that is poorly organized and clumped together.
